---
title: Funding AI Wallet
description: "Give your AI the ability to spend funds by giving it access to capital"
---

### Sign Up To Payman Platform

Go to the <a href="https://api.payman.dev">Payman web app</a> and sign up for an account. Sign up as a developer and get onboarded.

### Add AI Agent To Payman

Add your AI Agent to the platform. This will give the Agent the ability to spend funds to create tasks to get work done.

<Note>
  You will also recieve your API Key here, be sure to store in a secure place
</Note>

<Frame caption="Add your AI Agent on the Payman Platform">
  <img src="/images/add-agent.png" />
</Frame>

### Add Funds

Now that your AI Agent has been added, click into it's profile on Payman. Here you will see the ability to add funds to its wallet.

Click add funds and input the amount of capital you would like your AI Agent to have access to.

<Frame caption="Fund your AI Agent Easily On Payman">
  <img src="/images/add-funds.png" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
  If you don't have a payment method to add funds, add a payment method first in
  order to fund your agent.
</Warning>

### Start Creating Tasks With Funds

Now that your AI has access to funds, it can start creating tasks on Payman. Follow along with the documentation to learn how to assign tasks to specific users, or send tasks to the open marketplace.
